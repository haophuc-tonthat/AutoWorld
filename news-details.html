Automobili Lamborghini at Milano Design Week 2021 

6 September 2021

Automobili Lamborghini attends the renowned Milano Design Week with an exhibit of iconic vehicles and project collaborations with partners iGuzzini and Tecnomar. 

The space, located at Superstudio Più in Via Tortona 27, will host the historic Countach LP400 and its contemporary descendant, the brand-new Countach LPI 800-4 on its European premiere, as well as the Aventador Ultimae.

Another area will present the collaboration between Centro Stile Automobili Lamborghini and iGuzzini, who created a very special design lamp. Mya is a customizable desk lighting solution with high-end performance: if you will, a representation through illumination of Lamborghini’s exclusive vehicles.
 

Finally, visitors will also be able to admire a model reproduction of the Lamborghini 63 motor yacht—the result of an exclusive collaboration with Tecnomar. The luxury "sea vehicle” presents unique design and performance, along with details that speak of Lamborghini’s tradition of innovation and brilliant engineering solutions. 

If you’d like to be part of the Automobili Lamborghini experience at Milano Fuorisalone, the space will be open to the public as follows: 

- September 4, 2021 - from 3pm to 9pm

- September 5 to September 9, 2021 - from 11am to 9pm

- September 10, 2021 - from 11am to 5pm

Access will be available following registration on the Superstudio Più website , where the user will be subsequently provided with a QR code for entering the exhibition space.
